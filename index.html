Produkt

Buffer Protection UB will create a completely new beanie with a soft shell for children and young adults who choose to use a beanie over a helmet. The product can be used for sportswear and daily use, and will absorb shocks to the head in the event of a fall.

Release

Where can you buy it?

Buffer Protection UB is planning on releasing the full product by winter 2023/2024. We are currently in the process of creating a prototype.

Statistics on helmet use:

In Norway, 13,000 cyclists are injured each year - a third are children under 14. Ten percent of bicycle injuries are head injuries, and one in five bicycle injuries that are treated in hospital are head injuries.

is 10 times the risk of getting a head injury in connection with a bicycle accident if you don't wear a helmet. Among cyclists who do not wear a helmet, eight out of ten suffer a head injury in connection with cycling accidents.

Figures from the Oslo Emergency Medical Service show that it is head injuries and arm injuries that make up over 65 per cent of injuries. Of the most serious injuries, most injuries are to the knees and legs, followed by head injuries. It is young men who are most often injured on electric scooters. Of 286 injuries where the e-kick cyclists suffered head injuries, only 4 used helmets.
